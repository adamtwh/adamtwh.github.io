About Me

Sophomore at Singapore Management University studying Information Systems. Aside from web development, I am passionate in photography. Becoming a photographer gives you an entirely new perspective on everyday life. It lets you turn anything you find beautiful into a work of art. One photograph is powerful enough to not only remind you of an event or detail, but can bring you right back to the feelings, sounds, and even smells of the moment.


I have shot innumerable Pulitzer Prize winning photos which has given me worldwide fame and fortune. Many have said that my masterfully captured shots have something in it that goes beyond the ordinary. Those fortunate enough to have seen my work often find themselves overwhelmed by their emotions and shed grateful tears.
